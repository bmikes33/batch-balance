Our Story

Alayna — baker & founder

Batch & Balance started in our home where I love to bake. Everything is made from scratch in small batches — real ingredients, old-fashioned technique, no shortcuts. Whether it's a cozy night in or a thoughtful gift, every order is baked like it's going to our own family.
